So you want to be a Programmer?

Q: Are you more interested in Front-end or Back-end development?

Front-end developers focus on making things look awesome! They work primarily on layouts, navigation, colors and design.

Back-end developers focus on building the software that allows a business to function, and work with a wide variety of languages!
