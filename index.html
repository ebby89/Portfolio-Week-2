So you want to be a Programmer?

Take the quiz below to help determine which track at Epicodus would best suit your goals!

Q: Are you more interested in Front-end or Back-end development?

Front-end developers focus on making things look awesome! They work primarily on layouts, navigation, colors and design.

Back-end developers focus on building the software that allows a business to function, and work with a wide variety of languages!

Q: What is your ideal workplace environment?

There are a variety of options, each with their own benefits and drawbacks.

Large Companies Typically offer less flexibility to produce your own creative content.

Small Companies / Startups Typically offer more flexibility to produce creative content, but less structure and lower entry level compensation.

Q: Why are you interested in coding?

The all-important question!

Q: "Lone-wolf" or "Team-captain": What type of working/learning environment do you prefer?

Programmers can and do work in a variety of environments. You may find yourself working solo, in pairs, in teams, or a combination!

Q: Do you plan to work while attending Epicodus?

Epicodus students attend class from 8am-5pm daily, with short homework assignments to be completed at home.
